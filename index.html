Biography

I am proud to say I am a first-generation Latina graduate; I acquired my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at New York University. Navigating my family's challenges and successes has instilled in me a resilient work ethic and commitment to personal and professional growth. I strive for excellence and embrace new opportunities, as they have made me who I am today. My fields of interest are artificial intelligence and software engineering. I hope to develop my career in these areas. I am inspired by leaders in tech who have paved the way for diverse talent and aim to use my career to inspire younger generations.
